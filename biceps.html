Suggested Exercise For Biceps

  • concentration curl

    activating both the long head and short head of your biceps.

  • Inclined curls

    you're putting resistance on the biceps brachii, which in turn engages and tightens, a process called a concentric contraction.

  • Dumbbell curls

    heavy weights for low reps to build strength, or with lighter weights for high reps to build endurance.

Suggested Exercise For Chest

  • Dumbbell press

    The bench press uses your shoulders, triceps, forearms, lats, pecs, traps, rhomboids, and pretty much every muscle in your upper body

  • Dumbbell Fly

    Dumbbell flyes will work both of your chest muscles, the larger pectoralis major and the smaller pectoralis minor

  • Inclined dumbbell press

    The purpose of the incline press is to focus more of the work on the upper pecs.

Suggested Exercise For Back

  • Reverse fly

    Strong upper back muscles help balance your shoulder strength and help protect your shoulder from injury.

  • dumbbell rows

    Dumbbell rows put special emphasis on your back muscles, including the latissimus dorsi, posterior deltoids, teres major, trapezius, and rhomboids.

  • Back Extension

    Back extension exercises improve the stability and strength of your erector spinae muscles.

Suggested Exercise For Shoulder

  • Side raises

    Lateral raises muscles worked primarily include the lateral deltoid muscles.

  • Front raises

    . By working the stabilising muscles, front raises help to improve shoulder stability and mobility.

  • Shoulder Press

    The shoulder press is one of the best exercises for strengthening your shoulders and upper back.

Suggested Exercise For Triceps

  • Dumbbell KickBacks

    the dumbbell kickback activates the deltoids and the muscles of the upper and mid-back.

  • Lying Extension

    entire triceps muscle group in the upper arm. It works the triceps from the elbow all the way to the latissimus dorsi.

  • Overhead Extension

    An overhead tricep extension, also known as a dumbbell tricep extension, is an isolation exercise that targets your triceps muscles.
